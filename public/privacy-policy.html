Privacy Policy for B10 News

Last Updated: November 29, 2025

1. Introduction

B10 News ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the B10 News mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information through the App and how we safeguard your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Search history: When you search for news or categories
  • View history: Which news articles and videos you view
  • Preferences: Your selected news categories and preferences

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information: Device type, operating system, unique identifiers
  • Usage data: How you interact with the App (features used, time spent)
  • Location data: General location based on IP address (not precise location)
  • Analytics data: App performance, crashes, and user behavior

2.3 Third-Party Services

Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics:

  • Google Firebase collects usage data and crash reports
  • Data includes: app interactions, performance metrics, device info

YouTube API:

  • When you view YouTube videos in the App
  • YouTube collects viewing data per their privacy policy

Push Notifications & NotifyHound:

  • We send push notifications to notify you about new content
  • Your device token is registered with our notification service
  • NotifyHound service monitors RSS feeds for new YouTube videos and blog posts
  • Notifications are sent through Expo Push Notification service

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve the App's functionality
  • Understand user behavior and preferences
  • Fix technical issues and app crashes (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Send push notifications about new content (YouTube videos and blog posts)
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Send important updates about the App

4. Data Sharing

  • Firebase: Your data is shared with Google Firebase for analytics and crash reporting
  • YouTube: Your viewing data is shared with YouTube when you access videos
  • Expo: Your push notification token is shared with Expo for notification delivery
  • NotifyHound: RSS feed data is processed through NotifyHound service for content monitoring
  • Third-party service providers: Only as necessary for app functionality
  • Legal requirements: We may share data if required by law

5. Data Security

  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information
  • Your data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Firebase provides security measures per Google's security standards
  • No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure

6. Data Retention

  • Usage analytics: Retained for 14 months (Firebase default)
  • Crash reports: Retained for troubleshooting purposes
  • Search history: Stored locally on your device
  • Push notification tokens: Retained for notification delivery purposes
  • You can clear app data anytime from device settings

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics collection
